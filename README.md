# Liquid Civilization · 液体文明

A civilisation-scale, fully bilingual (EN | 中文) platform exploring beverages as chemistry,
biology, ritual, economy, psychology, and civilisation infrastructure.

> Civilisation can be read through what people drink. The history of beverages is the history
> of chemistry flowing through civilisation itself.
>
> 文明，可以透过人们喝什么来阅读。饮品的历史，就是化学流经文明本身的历史。

A global language toggle switches every label, map, timeline, diagram, and essay between
English and Chinese without a reload.

## The ten systems

1. **Global Beverage Map** — interactive trade map of drink origins + colonial routes
2. **Drink Evolution Timeline** — fermentation → tea → coffeehouses → distillation → soda → AI drinks
3. **Fermentation Engine** — interactive sim: beer/wine, yogurt/kefir, kombucha/vinegar
4. **Neurochemistry & the Body** — a heat-grid comparing caffeine, theanine, ethanol, sugar, taurine, cocoa
5. **The Tea Civilisation** — Chinese tea, Japanese chadō, the British empire
6. **The Coffeehouse Civilisation** — Ottoman cafés, the penny university, capitalism's fuel
7. **Alcohol & Social Systems** — beer democracy, wine hierarchy, ritual intoxication
8. **Sugar & Soft-Drink Empires** — plantations, carbonation, the Coca-Cola planet
9. **The Future Drink Lab** — nootropics, precision fermentation, personalised nutrition
10. **Drink Psychology & Identity** — wine elitism, café intellectualism, bubble-tea belonging

Plus a **Beverage Civilisation Influence** meta-model: a seven-axis radar scoring each drink on
neurochemical effect, scalability, social ritual value, preservation, trade economics, addictive
potential, and cultural symbolism.

Featured beverages: tea, coffee, beer, wine, distilled spirits, cacao, dairy ferments, soda,
energy drinks, and kombucha — each with signature compound, origin, process, neuro-effect, and
civilisational story.
